About Us

Welccome To Our Website

Our History e-Pharmacy and e-Surgery was founded in 2021 by healthcare professionals as a response to the huge amount of paper waste produced by the Maverick.
Outdated systems exist in both community pharmacies and hospitals meaning prescriptions are often still printed out at a high volume. In addition, the global pandemic has exacerbated the demand for accessible healthcare, a service that we felt was globally inadequate.
‘In my experience as a trained Maverick Doctor, I witnessed wasteful prescription practices and people waiting weeks for simple treatments.
My co-founder, working as an maverick pharmacist, also started to recognise the lack of maverick funding and cuts that had led to a decline in the services available to patients.
’ –Sajath Ahamed CEO ‘I was shocked by the waste created by inefficient prescription systems and made it my mission to find an eco-friendly approach to healthcare that worked for everyone!’
– Piriyakan COO.At e-Surgery, we are proud to be experts in sustainable health, with our paperless prescribing system offering affordable private healthcare from the comfort of your own home.
